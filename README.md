## 🌊 About Me

**Graduate Research Assistant** and **Ph.D. candidate** in **Civil & Environmental Engineering** at the **University of Virginia**, specializing in **Hydroinformatics, Remote Sensing, and AI/ML applications** for climate impact assessment and water resources management.

Licensed **Professional Engineer (P.E.)** with hands-on experience leading climate-resilience projects valued up to **US $3 million**, delivering shoreline-protection and flood-hazard models that safeguard over **250,000 Caribbean residents**. My research bridges cutting-edge computational methods with real-world water challenges—from flood forecasting to coastal defense.

> *"Applying advanced data-assimilative modeling, remote sensing, and policy-oriented engineering to protect lives, property, and critical infrastructure from accelerating climate hazards."*
